About me


I am a designer, interested in how design research and cross disciplinary collaboration can help answer complicated problems and meet users needs. I am curious about how technology shapes our behaviours as we have been shaping it; and what the designers role will be as new technologies are integrated into our vital services.

I am currently working at the Home Office Digital completing the Interaction Design Internship program this autumn. Previously I worked for four years across the areas of graphic design, art direction and design installations.

I completed my MA in Interaction Design at Goldsmiths in December 2016, and BA Visual Communication at Glasgow School of Art in 2011.
